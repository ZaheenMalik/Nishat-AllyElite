    Our Core Competencies

    Analytics

    While data has the potential to become one of an enterprise’s most valuable resources unfortunately, many enterprise's are simply drowning in this data. They face challenges managing, integrating, and securing such a large volume and variety of data, and aren’t able to leverage it effectively to their advantage. It is a challenge for enterprises to extract accurate and timely insights from data residing in different formats and in siloed processes. To achieve the most out of the information that you have, you need to reimagine & explore the possibilities that you can unlock. ALLY-ELITE solutions help enterprises develop conclusive fact-based strategies, empowering users and delivering a competitive edge.Our range of analytics services helps uncover historical insights by streamlining data from multiple source systems within enterprises and organizes the insights in interactive and flexible visualization that empowers users to derive intelligence.

    Business Performance Management

    Monitor performance and react instantly to issues as they arise with the alignment of your organisation's objectives, strategy, planning, forecasting, performance metrics and data systems in a real-time view with ALLY-ELITE. We help companies with strategy development, goal alignment, operations planning, monitoring as well as testing & adaptation for a thorough analysis of your business performance. Our EPM services and solutions make a big difference in our clients’ strategy, profitability, cost management, business planning, financial consolidation and spend and supply chain.

    Data Engineering

    Delivering data management framework that is aligned with the organization's tactical and strategic needs is crucial for businesses today to succeed with their analytics vision.We deliver robust, scalable and secure on-premise, cloud or hybrid data management solutions aligned with your business objectives. Our services encompass the entire spectrum of data and its processing for business decision-making including. We offer Data Warehouse,Big Data,Data Lake.We provide organizations with cutting-edge data management solutions by taking into account their existing business, data and technology stack such as their data architecture, digital maturity and hosting environment.

    Offshore Development Centre

    Establishing your new team all starts with choosing the best offshore development partner.With a mission to enable enterprises to stay on track with their digital transformation journey, our Offshore Development Centre (ODC) offers dedicated as well as flexible teams & infrastructure. We are helping organisations restore this focus on what they do the best, while our team works as an extended arm to tackle the rest.
